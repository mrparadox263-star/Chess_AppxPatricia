About the Patricia Engine

Ever wondered what it's like to play against a neural network? This app lets you challenge Patricia, a powerful custom-compiled NNUE (Efficiently Updatable Neural Network) chess engine. Instead of relying purely on old-school brute-force calculations, Patricia evaluates positions using advanced AI architecture, making her play style incredibly sharp, dynamic, and surprisingly human.

I built this entire experience from the ground up to bring the engine directly to your browser. Under the hood, the raw C++ engine is hooked up to a custom Python Flask API, containerized with Docker, and optimized for seamless mobile and desktop play. Whether you are looking for a casual warmup at the 600 Elo beginner setting or a brutal tactical test at the 2400 Master level, the board is ready. Pick your color, dial in the difficulty, and see if you can outsmart the algorithm!
